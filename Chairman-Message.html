It is indeed a great pleasure to welcome you to Anna University Sports Board. We at Sports Board are committed to put our best in development of sports at all levels like Zonal / Inter Zonal / Inter University and International. Anna University Sports Board strives to identify and nurture the talents among the young students and enable them to be great sportsmen.

Anna University's priority is to identify young talents who excel in sports to represent the University at State / National / Inter-National levels and get laurels to the University, State and National. The affiliated colleges are the supporting pillars of the Anna University Sports Board.

We implement programmes like Paralympic meet to give an opportunity to the physically challenged students to exhibit the sporting talents.

I wish the aspiring students to make use of the opportunities rendered by the Sports Board to enrich their sports skill in the chosen field for their individual growth which enables them to bring laurels to their Institution and the University as well, ultimately raising our Universities profile in sports arena.
